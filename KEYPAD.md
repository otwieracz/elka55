*X*: 8, B, E, F  
*Y*: 7, 9, 10, 11, C, D, G, I

	* | E  | F  | B  | 8  |
	--+----+----+----+----+
	7 | M≡ | #  | C  | M- |
	9 | ÷  | 5  | R  | 4  |
	10| M* | 00 | ±  | ,  |
	11| %  | 7  | ⋄  | 6  |
	C | IC | 1  | +  | 0  |
	D | –  | 3  | ×  | 2  |
	G | PF | 9  | M⋄ | 8  |
	I |*/= | M+ | M± |    |

note:	M± should be M-PLUS SIGN ABOVE EQUALS SIGN
	(M and U+2A72 [M-PLUS-EQUALS])
